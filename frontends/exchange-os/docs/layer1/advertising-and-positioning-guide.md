# Advertising and Positioning Guide

**Document**: TSN Communications Series
**Status**: Approved internal guidance — review with legal before external publication

---

## Purpose

This document provides approved and forbidden language for communicating about the Troptions Settlement Network (TSN) to investors, partners, developers, and the public. It is designed to support accurate, responsible marketing that avoids regulatory, legal, and reputational risk.

---

## What TSN Is

TSN is a **compliance-native simulation environment and institutional settlement network architecture**. It models regulated financial operations — stablecoin issuance, trustline settlement, RWA tokenization, cross-rail routing — with compliance gates at every layer.

The current scaffold is **simulation-only**. No live stablecoin issuance, no live settlement, no live token transfers, no banking integration is active.

Every operation flows through the Troptions Compliance Scoring Algorithm (TCSA) and the Troptions Control Hub before any state change is authorized. The network is designed for GENIUS Act alignment, MiCA-aware operations, FATF/AML readiness, and OFAC sanctions compliance — with legal review required before any live asset issuance.

## Safer Language Guide

When describing compliance capabilities, prefer precision over superlatives:

| Instead of... | Use this instead... |
|---|---|
| "Fully compliant" | "Designed for alignment with..." |
| "Regulatory approved" | "Designed with regulatory readiness in mind, pending legal review" |
| "GENIUS Act compliant" | "Designed for GENIUS Act alignment" |
| "MiCA compliant" | "MiCA-aware architecture" |
| "AML compliant" | "AML-ready with FATF-aligned controls" |
| "OFAC cleared" | "OFAC sanctions screening logic implemented" |
| "Bank integrated" | "Designed for future bank rail integration" |
| "CBDC compatible" | "Architected for compatibility with CBDC infrastructure models" |
| "Quantum-proof" | "Quantum-resistant roadmap aligned to NIST FIPS 203/204/205" |
| "Guaranteed returns" | Never say — not allowed in any context |
| "Risk-free" | Never say — not allowed in any context |

## Forbidden Language

The following phrases MUST NOT be used in any marketing, investor, partner, or public communication:

| Phrase | Why It Is Forbidden |
|---|---|
| "Fully globally compliant" | No system is globally compliant with all regulations in all jurisdictions — this is false and potentially illegal |
| "Guaranteed GENIUS Act approved" | The GENIUS Act establishes requirements; TSN is not approved by any government entity |
| "Central-bank integrated" | TSN has no integration with any central bank — simulation adapters are not integrations |
| "Replaces SWIFT" | An unsubstantiated and potentially false competitive claim |
| "Guaranteed liquidity" | Market liquidity cannot be guaranteed — this is a securities law risk |
| "Quantum-proof today" | Current system uses Ed25519/secp256k1 — not quantum-resistant |
| "SEC registered" | Not registered with the SEC — do not imply otherwise |
| "FDIC insured" | Never — not applicable to digital assets |
| "No risk" or "risk-free" | Explicitly prohibited in financial communications under SEC, FTC, and consumer protection law |
| "Guaranteed yield" or "guaranteed APY" | Prohibited — implies investment contract, may trigger securities regulation |
| "BIS approved" or "Fed approved" | Not approved by BIS, Federal Reserve, or any government body |

For competitive positioning against Avalanche, Cardano, XRPL, and Stellar:

> **vs. Avalanche**: "TSN prioritizes institutional compliance and regulatory readiness over throughput maximization. Where Avalanche optimizes for 4,500 TPS, TSN optimizes for audit-complete, compliance-checked settlement operations."

> **vs. Cardano**: "TSN shares Cardano's formal methods emphasis but focuses on regulatory compliance rather than academic proof. Where Cardano prioritizes peer review, TSN prioritizes institutional operator usability."

> **vs. XRPL**: "XRPL provides efficient payment rails with mature trustline and DEX infrastructure. TSN models compatibility with XRPL through a simulation adapter while adding compliance gate enforcement that XRPL itself does not enforce at the protocol level."

> **vs. Stellar**: "Stellar enables fast, low-cost cross-border payments and asset issuance. TSN treats Stellar as a cross-rail target through a simulation adapter while adding GENIUS Act-aligned stablecoin governance that Stellar's anchor model does not provide natively."

## Tone Guidance

**TSN is serious institutional infrastructure. Match the tone.**

- Be precise, not hyperbolic
- Cite specific algorithms, standards, and specifications
- Acknowledge what is not done — this builds trust with institutional partners
- Do not oversell the current state — the architecture is the asset, not the hype

## Review Requirements

Before any external publication:
1. Legal review of all compliance and regulatory claims
2. Ensure no securities law implications (e.g., investment contract under Howey test)
3. Verify no GENIUS Act or banking regulation claims have changed since this document was written
4. Confirm TSN simulation status accurately represented (simulation-only until explicitly changed)
