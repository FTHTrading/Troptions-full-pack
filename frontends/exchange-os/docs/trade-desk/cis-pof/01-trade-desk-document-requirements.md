## Freshness Baseline
1. Bank/custodian statements: typically not older than 30 days unless desk policy says otherwise.
2. Government IDs: must be unexpired.
3. Good standing and registry extracts: typically not older than 90 days.
4. Screening checks: rerun on material change or before final approval.

## Redaction Policy Baseline
1. Allow masking account numbers except last 4 digits.
2. Do not redact legal entity name, institution name, statement date, currency, amount, issuer/beneficiary identity, or signatures.
3. Redactions that remove critical verification context are reject conditions.

## Signoff Chain
1. Intake reviewer
2. Compliance reviewer
3. Trade desk reviewer
4. Final approver / delegated signatory
