# Troptions Brand System

_Last audited: 2026-04-27 — site audit of troptions.org, troptionsxchange.io, and troptions-unitytoken.com._

---

## Master Logo — Canonical Reference

**The master TROPTIONS logo** is the gold double-T serif mark paired with the "TROPTIONS" wordmark on a white/ivory background.

- Mark: Two overlapping serif "T" glyphs rendered in a warm gold-to-amber metallic gradient.
- Wordmark: "TROPTIONS" set in a classical serif typeface, bold weight, black ink.
- Background: White (#FFFFFF) or Ivory (#F7F2E8) only for the canonical version.
- File naming convention: `troptions-logo-master-[light|dark]-[size].{svg|png}`
- Source files must be placed in: `public/troptions/brand/`

**Do not fabricate logo files from memory.** Obtain original source files from the brand owner before publishing to new surfaces.

## Logo Usage

- Use mark and wordmark on Deep Navy or Ivory backgrounds.
- Preserve clear-space equal to at least one mark-height on all sides.
- Do not stretch, rotate, or apply glow effects.

## Colors

- Deep Navy: #071426
- Ink Black: #0B0B0D
- Institutional Gold: #C9A24A
- Muted Gold: #A8873D
- Ivory: #F7F2E8
- Paper White: #FFFDF8
- Slate: #233044

## Typography

- Headings: Serif family for annual-report tone.
- Body: Legible sans-serif for operational readability.
- Numerical tables: Monospace only where alignment is needed.

## Old-Money Design Language

- Sober layouts and report-style cards.
- Evidence-first hierarchy.
- Minimal motion and no speculative visuals.
- Governance and controls foregrounded over promotion.

## Acceptable Language

- proof-gated infrastructure
- custody-aware operations
- board-approved release
- evidence before issuance
- governance before settlement
- documented value
- capital discipline
- long-term stewardship

## Banned Hype Language

- guaranteed returns
- risk-free
- moon
- instant liquidity
- fully compliant everywhere
- world-changing
- revolutionary
- bank replacement
- exchange replacement

## Institutional Tone Rules

- Use neutral, verifiable language.
- No claims without evidence links.
- No compliance claims without legal review.
- No liquidity or redemption promises.
- Always include jurisdiction and approval qualifiers.

## Trademark Status

- The TROPTIONS name and double-T mark are claimed trade marks of their respective owner.
- **Trademark registration is pending confirmation.** Do not publish "® Registered" until registration is confirmed by counsel.
- Until confirmed, use "™" (unregistered mark) on all new material.
- The logo files must not be licenced, sub-licenced, or white-labelled without written authorisation from the brand owner.
- Any partner or affiliate usage of the TROPTIONS name or mark must include: (a) the brand owner attribution, (b) the appropriate ™ symbol, and (c) a written use-authorisation reference.
