# Third-Party And Affiliate Risk

Affiliate or related third-party stablecoin-holder rewards create a strong risk that prohibited yield is simply being routed through another entity.

## Automatic Red Flags

- Stablecoin holder gets value only because they hold or use the stablecoin.
- Affiliate funds or arranges the reward.
- Marketing language suggests APY, passive income, or guaranteed return.

## Response

Classify as high risk, block launch claims, escalate to legal counsel, and propose a safer structure.
