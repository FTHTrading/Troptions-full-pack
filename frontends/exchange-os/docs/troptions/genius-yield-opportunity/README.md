# Troptions GENIUS Yield Opportunity Engine

This module is a legal and compliance-first opportunity scanner for credit-union, CUSO, tokenized-deposit, merchant-rebate, public-chain, and RWA settlement structures.

## Payment Stablecoin Lane

- Settlement and payment only.
- No issuer-paid yield to holders.
- Not insured to the holder.
- Live issuance blocked until regulated issuer or approved partner controls exist.

## Tokenized Deposit Lane

- Separate from payment stablecoins.
- Controlled by a regulated financial institution.
- May support interest or dividend under banking or credit-union rules.
- Must not be marketed as stablecoin yield.

## Merchant Rebate Lane

- Commerce-linked rewards may be safer when merchant-funded and transaction-specific.
- Balance-based or holding-time rewards are blocked or high risk.

## Affiliate And Third-Party Risk

- High risk when the issuer or an affiliate funds holder rewards tied to holding, using, or retaining a stablecoin.
- Troptions should classify and block these patterns rather than market them.

## Troptions Revenue Model

Troptions monetizes software, compliance automation, routing, namespace orchestration, partner packets, dashboards, and settlement tooling. It does not monetize holder APY.
