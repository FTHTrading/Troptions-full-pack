# RWA And Private Market Guardrails

## Stablecoin As Payment Rail Only

Troptions models the stablecoin lane as settlement infrastructure only. It does not convert the payment rail into an investment, yield, or guaranteed redemption product.

## Review Requirements

- Legal review per product
- Securities review where applicable
- Investor or accreditation checks where applicable
- Escrow and evidence requirements
- Marketing review for every claim touching yield, return, or redemption

## Prohibited Claims

- Guaranteed yield
- Guaranteed return
- Stablecoin insured to holder
- Stablecoin backed by private-market performance
- Automatic redemption promises without lawful support

## Evidence And Audit Requirements

- Product memo
- Legal review record
- Eligibility review record
- Escrow workflow evidence
- Marketing copy approval
