# Merchant Settlement Playbook

## Covered Flows

- Local merchants
- Contractors and vendors
- Private-market participants
- Escrow workflows
- Invoice settlement

## How Troptions Creates Value Before Live Issuance

- Namespace onboarding and merchant identity mapping
- Compliance evidence collection
- Settlement workflow design and visibility
- Partner routing and diligence packet generation
- Simulated ledger and redemption-request flows

## Live Constraint

Every merchant or contractor money movement path remains blocked until regulated issuer, reserve, redemption, AML/KYC, sanctions, and board approvals are complete.
