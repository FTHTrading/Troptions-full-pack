# Compliance Gates

Each gate can block sandbox, partner-ready, regulator-ready, or live status depending on level.

## Gate Inventory

- Legal counsel memo: live blocker until approved.
- Board approval: live blocker until approved.
- Regulator approval: live blocker until approved.
- Reserve policy: partner blocker until approved.
- Reserve custody agreement: regulator blocker until approved.
- Reserve attestation: live blocker when missing or expired.
- Redemption policy: partner blocker until approved.
- AML/BSA policy: sandbox blocker until approved.
- Sanctions/OFAC policy: sandbox blocker until approved.
- KYC/KYB provider: sandbox blocker until approved.
- Chain analytics provider: live blocker until approved.
- Smart contract audit: live blocker until approved.
- Cybersecurity plan: regulator blocker until approved.
- Incident response plan: partner blocker until approved.
- Consumer disclosures: live blocker until approved.
- Marketing review: regulator blocker until approved.
- RWA/securities review: regulator blocker until approved.
- Tax/accounting review: partner blocker until approved.

## Blocking Effect

- Sandbox actions are permitted only when sandbox-level controls are approved.
- Partner readiness requires sandbox gates plus partner-level operational controls.
- Regulator readiness requires partner gates plus deeper governance, reserve, and disclosure controls.
- Live enablement requires every live gate plus a licensed or approved partnered issuer path.
