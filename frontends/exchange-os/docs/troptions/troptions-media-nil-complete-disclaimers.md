## Media Rights & Signature Engine

### What Signature Engine Is

The Media Rights & Signature Engine is a document management and signature collection simulation system that:

- Creates and tracks media rights agreements
- Collects signatures from multiple signatories
- Records guardian consent for minors
- Validates agreement execution conditions
- Generates compliance-ready packets

**Signature Engine is NOT:**

- A legally binding digital signature system
- A contract execution platform
- A blockchain-anchored notarization service
- A regulatory filing system
- A compliance guarantee
- A legal advice provider

### Signature Validity

All signatures collected in this system are **simulation only** and do **NOT** create legal obligations.

To be legally binding, agreements must:

- Be reviewed by legal counsel (both parties)
- Be signed through a certified digital signature provider
- Comply with state/federal electronic signature laws (ESIGN, UETA)
- Include proper consideration and intent language
- Be countersigned by authorized agents
- Be retained with full audit trail and timestamps
- Comply with industry-specific regulations (if applicable)

### Guardian Consent for Minors

Before any minor can participate in NIL, media, or campaign systems:

1. Parent/guardian MUST review all terms
2. Parent/guardian MUST understand all risks
3. Parent/guardian MUST sign consent using certified platform
4. Consent MUST be reversible (minor/guardian can withdraw)
5. All communications MUST be transparent to guardian
6. Guardian MUST retain all documentation
7. Minor's privacy MUST be protected
8. Educational benefit MUST be demonstrated
9. No financial inducement MUST be present

**If guardian consent is not genuine or documented:**

- All agreements are void
- System access is revoked
- Creator records are flagged for review
- Sponsor may face liability
