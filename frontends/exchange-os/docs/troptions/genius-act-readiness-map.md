## What the GENIUS Act Is

The Guiding and Establishing National Innovation for US Stablecoins (GENIUS) Act is proposed US federal legislation for payment stablecoin regulation. It establishes:

- Reserve requirements (1:1 high-quality liquid assets)
- Issuer licensing (federal or state)
- Redemption rights
- Anti-money laundering obligations
- Disclosure requirements

The GENIUS Act applies to **payment stablecoin issuers**. TROPTIONS is evaluating its operations against GENIUS Act criteria for readiness purposes only.
