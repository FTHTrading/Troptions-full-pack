TROPTIONS Full DAO — Governance Knowledge Base

OVERVIEW
The Full DAO is a production-grade sovereign stack: L1-native proposals and soulbound-weighted voting, Python orchestration on ports 8091/8093, multi-chain treasury visibility, and real-time WebSocket updates.

L1 RPC (port 9944)
Query: state_get, soulbound_get, soulbound_by_owner, settlement_get, balance_get, governance_get, proposal_get, proposal_list
Submit: submit_soulbound_mint, submit_namespace_register, submit_proposal_create, submit_proposal_vote, submit_proposal_finalize, submit_proposal_execute

PROPOSAL LIFECYCLE
1. submit_proposal_create — proposer must be 32-byte hex account
2. Voting window — soulbound credentials determine weight (non-revoked tokens per owner)
3. submit_proposal_vote — choice: for, against, abstain
4. submit_proposal_finalize — after voting_ends_at block height
5. Timelock — passed proposals wait DEFAULT_TIMELOCK_BLOCKS
6. submit_proposal_execute — marks Executed

QUORUM
10% of active soulbound credentials must participate; majority for wins.
